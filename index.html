About the Company

Fika is a concept, a state of mind, an attitude and an important part of Swedish culture. Many Swedes consider that it is almost essential to make time for fika every day. It means making time for friends and colleagues to share a cup of coffee (or tea) and a little something to eat. Our company follows these principles. We find relaxation an peace in drinking coffee together with friends and family; a moment to enjoy.
